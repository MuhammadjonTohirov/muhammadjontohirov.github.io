Frequently Asked Questions

What does this app do?

PDF Booklet Maker converts standard PDF documents into printable booklet formats. It supports 2-in-1 (standard booklet) and 4-in-1 (pocket booklet) page arrangements.

How do I use the app?

  1. Open a PDF file
  2. Choose a booklet layout (2-up or 4-up)
  3. Optionally add a cover image
  4. Tap "Reorder Pages" to convert
  5. Export, print, or share your booklet

Is my data safe?

Yes. All PDF processing happens entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I restore my purchase?

If you previously purchased the 4-in-1 Booklet feature, tap the locked 4-in-1 option and then select "Restore Purchases" on the purchase screen.

The app isn't working correctly

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, please contact us using the information below.
