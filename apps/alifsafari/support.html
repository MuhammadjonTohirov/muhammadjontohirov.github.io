Frequently Asked Questions

What does this app do?

Alif Safari helps children (and curious adults) learn the Arabic alphabet through interactive tracing, native pronunciation, and playful quizzes. It covers all 28 letters with their isolated, initial, medial, and final forms.

How do I get started?

  1. Open the app and pick a letter from the home screen
  2. Listen to the pronunciation and watch the stroke animation
  3. Trace the letter with your finger or Apple Pencil
  4. Test what you've learned with a quiz
  5. Earn stars and keep your learning streak going

Is it safe for children?

Yes. Alif Safari is designed to be kid-safe: no ads, no third-party tracking, and no data collection. Everything works offline on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Does it work offline?

Yes. All letters, audio, and quizzes are bundled with the app and work without an internet connection.

How do I restore my purchase?

If you previously unlocked Alif Safari Pro, open the app, go to the purchase or settings screen, and tap "Restore Purchases".

The app isn't working correctly

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, please contact us using the information below and include your device model and iOS version.
