Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

TOX LLC ("we", "our", or "us") built Alif Safari as a commercial application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

Information Collection and Use

Alif Safari does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All learning activity, progress tracking, and audio playback happen entirely on your device. We do not have access to anything you or your child does inside the app.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics services, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies. The app contains no third-party SDKs that collect user data.

Data Storage

Progress, stars, and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not upload, share, or transfer any of this data to any server or third party.

Children's Privacy

Alif Safari is designed with children in mind. The app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. There are no ads, no in-app messaging, and no links to external content aimed at children.

In-App Purchases

Alif Safari may offer optional in-app purchases. These are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not see or store any payment information.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services that collect personal information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
